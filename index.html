Abby Truitt

Author and Stylist

Abby is a mother of two, teacher, visual stylist, designer, and lover of vibrant colors and subtle details. I created the book I wish I had growing up when I wanted to learn all about sewing and fashion design.

I want to inspire all children to love the feel of fabric, the shimmer of a button, and the hum of a sewing machine.

Carol Vaziri

Photography

Carol is a San Francisco-based photographer specializing in lifestyle work, encompassing everything from commercial and editorial projects to portraits and interiors.

Most importantly she is an active mother of three with a join-the-fun attitude toward childhood play and discovery.

Amanda Gross Tuft

Layout and Typography

Amanda has been working in the creative field as a designer and photographer for over 20 years. She works with a range of clients in the technology, entertainment, and lifestyle fields.

She currently lives in San Francisco wth her husband, two children, and a perpetually overflowing art closet.

An Invitation to Sew is the ideal gift and guide for every child who loves fashion design and sewing

This book stitches together all things fabric, ribbons, and lace that I loved as a child with the knowledge and wisdom I acquired as an adult.

It inspires girls and boys as our young seamstress creates a dress from drawing to sewing. Woven through her journey are facts about fibers, fabrics, tools, and sewing techniques. The vibrant colors and intricate images in the book are eye-candy for all to enjoy.

It's only one in a series of planned books on cooking, jewelry making, fiber arts, photography, and more. How-to books with supporting products and services are a distinct possibility too. ~ Abby
